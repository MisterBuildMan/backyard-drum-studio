### Project Scope Summary

**Included in scope:**
- Permanent accessory structure for music studio use
- Concrete slab foundation
- Sound-isolated construction (target STC 55-60)
- HVAC system
- Electrical service (lighting, outlets, HVAC power)
- Interior finish (drywall, flooring)
- Acoustic treatment

**Explicitly NOT in scope:**
- Plumbing / bathroom
- Kitchen facilities
- Sleeping accommodations (would trigger ADU requirements)
